WHO IS
BEHIND THIS

40+ Years. 500+ Clients. Zero Shortcuts.

I wasn’t always the coach people know today. I started competing in bodybuilding as a teenager in 1986 and decades later, I am still competing at the highest level in my 50s. Over the years, I became known as “the trainer to the trainers” by mentoring fitness professionals and helping clients build lasting strength, discipline, and confidence.

My journey has taken me from Melbourne to New York, earning titles including AAU Natural Mr. Universe Masters Champion, IFBB Natural North American Champion, and NABBA Mr. Australia. I have spent years refining my approach through real world experience in bodybuilding, nutrition, athletic performance, and coaching.

Beyond competition, I hold a Bachelor of Physical Education and multiple certifications in fitness coaching, sports performance, nutrition, and personal fitness chef training. My coaching combines elite level training knowledge with practical nutrition strategies built from decades inside the industry.

I work with a limited number of clients to ensure every program is personalized, results driven, and built for long term success.

Common Questions

FAQ

Every question you're thinking — answered directly.

How fast will I see results?
+

Most clients notice visible changes within the first 3–4 weeks. Significant transformation — the kind that turns heads — typically takes 10–16 weeks depending on your starting point and consistency. We will be honest with you upfront about what's realistic for your body and timeline.

Do I need a gym membership or equipment?
+

Gym access gives you the best results, but we can absolutely write programs around a home setup with minimal equipment. Your program is built around what YOU have available — this is stated clearly in your application.

I'm a complete beginner — is this right for me?
+

Yes. Beginners often see the most dramatic transformations because their bodies respond so strongly to a well-structured program. We start where you are and build from there. No experience required — just commitment.

I'm really busy. Will I have time for this?
+

Our busiest clients — CEOs, doctors, engineers working 60+ hour weeks — have gotten incredible results. We design your training around your actual schedule. Three 45-minute sessions per week is often enough to produce dramatic changes when the programming is dialed in.

What's the cost of coaching?
+

Pricing is discussed on the strategy call after your application is approved. What we can say: it's a significant investment in yourself, and every client who commits fully gets results that justify that investment many times over. We don't publish pricing publicly because every program is custom-built.

What if I don't see results?
+

If you follow the program as prescribed and don't see meaningful progress, we'll work with you at no extra cost until you do. In 8 years of coaching, this has never happened when a client followed the plan with genuine effort. Results are not optional — they're the whole point.
